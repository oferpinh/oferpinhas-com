About

Composer, arranger and musical director working between the concert hall and the bandstand. Rooted in classical training, at home in jazz, folklore and world music. One goal in every project: music that moves the body as much as it sharpens the mind.

Graduate of the Buchmann-Mehta School of Music, Tel Aviv University · studied with Prof. Yitzhak Sadai · works performed by the Revolution Orchestra, Ensemble Meitar, the Galilee Chamber Orchestra and the oneMusic Orchestra.
